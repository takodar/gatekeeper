Company Description

AuthEntry is a leading technology company and only provider to successfully combine three distinct technologies into one scalable and cost effective solution. The company specializes in delivering a federated approach to tokenless and token-based Multi-Factor Authentication, Identify Access Management and Two-Factor Secure Desktop Login, in one seamless solution. The company’s patent-pending technology places it at the forefront of mitigating the inherent risks associated with weak Identity Access practices and antiquated usernames and passwords for entry into consumer or corporate applications, networks and online accounts accessed through desktops, laptops, or mobile devices.

Company Mission

AuthEntry’s mission is to eliminate the occurrence of password breaches for consumers and corporations through provisioning the industry’s most powerful Identity Management System. Our vision is to be recognized as the performance leader in the Identity Management industry, and maintain our position by providing the most technically advanced, easy to use, cost effective solutions that deliver security and return on investment.
