If you aren't using a password manager, you need to start now. It's an easy way to organize and protect your online life. Do it today.

The recently discovered Heartbleed bug and daily occurrences of personal data theft makes using a strong, unique password for every site even more imperative.

Online merchants and secure websites aren't doing a good enough job of keeping your personal information safe. Even those sites using decent security practices may have been compromised by the recently discovered Heartbleed bug and other daily online hacking activities. If the bad guys nab your password, you're in trouble. But if you used that same password at other sites, you're really in trouble. You need to use a different strong password on every site, and the only practical way to do that is with a two-factor password manager like GateKeeper Pro.

Don’t worry, you only have to remember your one Master Password. We do all the rest!

GateKeeper Pro supports authentication using a master password. Since it's protecting all of your other passwords, that one needs to be really strong. But if that's the only protection for your data, a crook who manages to steal your master password has the keys to your kingdom. That’s why we offer two-factor authentication, something you know, your master password and something you have: a physical device like the LogiKey or a mobile two factor generator like our MPV. It's an added layer of security on all of your accounts so you can be confident that no one else can get into your accounts.
