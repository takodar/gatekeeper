    That is an excellent question, Indeed :)

With our GateKeeper Pro Two-factor password manager your accounts are safer than ever. GateKeeper Pro is the most convenient, easy to use and affordable password manager ever! Its as easy as 123, Here’s how it works.

  • Sign up for GateKeeper Pro NOW and enter all of your favorite online accounts from any device.    Click here for Plans & Pricing
  • Create ultra secure passwords for each individual account with our convenient Secure Password Generator feature. The longer and more complex your password is the better to keep the bad guys out. Don’t worry, we remember all of them and give you only one Mater Password you have to remember for all your accounts. Simple and secure !
  • Add a second layer of protection when you log into your accounts with our Mobile Passcode Verification feature. Simply click on the GateKeeper Pro cloud icon on your mobile device and we instantly give you a One time, One use secret passcode number to use in addition to your password. So even if the bad guys somehow get your password, they still need your mobile device to login. Security Plus!
